2

What VETsage Is

  • AI-powered prep tool for veterinarians
  • NOT a practice management system (PIMS)
  • Takes messy records → organized clarity
  • Designed for use BEFORE consultations
3

How It Works

  • Upload patient files (PDFs, images, records)
  • AI processes and creates a 'Deck'
  • Get summaries, key insights, timelines
  • Links back to original source documents
  • Zero setup — just drag, drop, and read
4

The Value Proposition

  • Save an entire workday every month
  • Cut prep time by 80%
  • 400+ hours saved by beta vets in 1 month
  • Real, measurable time savings
  • Proven with actual users
5

The Philosophy

  • "Enhances clarity, not control"
  • "Remove the mess, not replace your mind"
  • AI handles sorting, scanning, organizing
  • Vet stays in charge of diagnosis & treatment
  • Human-enhancing, not human-replacing
6

Competitive Landscape

  • Full PIMS: Digitail, Shepherd (everything)
  • AI Scribes: Nabla, Suki (during consults)
  • VETsage: Unique prep-focused niche
  • Not trying to be everything
  • Specifically for understanding records
7

The Unique Position

  • Other tools: Generate docs DURING consult
  • VETsage: Prepare BEFORE consult
  • Handles transfers, referrals, multi-year histories
  • Reading time: 20-30 min → 5 min (80% faster)
  • Complements existing practice software
8

Target Audience

  • Vets handling referral cases
  • Complex chronic patients
  • Multi-vet clinics with constant handoffs
  • Emergency & specialty practices
  • 15-25 patients/day, values clinical thinking
